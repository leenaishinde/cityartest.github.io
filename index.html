Welcome to CITYARCH Engineering Consultants

    

We are proud to introduce ourselves as Engineering Consultants based in Dubai, UAE. We are committed to provide high quality of professional service for our clients. We work to create attractive, decorative and economical designs as per client’s requirements. We offer vide range of architectural, structural, MEP, interior design services with consultation, project planning, project management, project estimation, pre-design building assessments and feasibility studies. We strive hard to provide strategic, flexible and innovative approach for design, engineering and management consultancy services for our clients.

All our projects are led by a highly experienced project team with a passion for delivering high quality commercial engineering solutions alongside an excellent level of service. Our expertise provides full project design services from concept design to construction supervision till completion of the project and handover to the client. Our prime motto is to provide unique and satisfactory, economical designs
