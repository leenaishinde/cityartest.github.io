About Us

CITYARCH Engineering Consultants is a multidisciplinary practice of Consulting Engineers providing a full range of Architectural, Structural, MEP, Interior Design, Project Management and Tendering Services.

We are fast growing company with innovative designs and cost-effective solutions. We have a highly qualified and professional engineering staff.

About

Managing Director

Mr. Indrajeet Shinde (M.E.Structures) is founder of CITYARCH Engineering Consultants , has over 20 years’ experience in Structural designing & Execution of Residential towers, Commercial buildings, Labor accommodation’s, Steel warehouses, Factories & Villa’s in India & UAE.
